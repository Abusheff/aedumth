Association Of Resident Doctors(ARD), UMTH

Taking health as a priority to human developement

Association of Resident Doctors – University Of Maiduguri Teaching Hospital (ARD – UMTH) is a body representing over 200 Medical Doctors undergoing specialist and super-specialist training in various fields of Medicine. It was established in the 1980s with the core objectives of advancing knowledge and promoting improved and quality health services to all Nigerians among other values.
